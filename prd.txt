# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Protein & Fitness Laddoo – Customizable Nutrition Website

---

## 1. Product Overview

### Product Name (Working)

Protein & Fitness Laddoo (name TBD)

### Vision

Create a visually rich, mobile-first website where users can:

* Buy **predefined fitness laddoos** (traditional + modern nutrition)
* **Customize laddoos** by ingredient proportions
* Clearly understand **nutrition, protein, vitamins, and benefits**

The product should feel:

* Premium, warm, Indian
* Fitness-focused but not clinical
* Fun, interactive, and trustworthy

---

## 2. Target Users

### Primary Users

* Gym-goers & fitness enthusiasts
* Health-conscious professionals (25–45)

### Secondary Users

* Families seeking healthier sweets
* Corporate gifting buyers

---

## 3. Core User Goals

Users should be able to:

1. Quickly understand **what makes these laddoos different**
2. Choose laddoos based on **fitness goals** (muscle, recovery, energy)
3. Trust the **nutrition & ingredient transparency**
4. Customize a laddoo without confusion
5. Order in kilos with clear pricing

---

## 4. Key Features (High Level)

### 4.1 Homepage

**Goal:** Capture attention and explain value within 5 seconds

Requirements:

* Hero section with premium laddoo imagery
* Clear headline (protein + tradition)
* CTAs: "Build Your Laddoo" and "Explore Fitness Laddoos"
* Goal-based entry points (Muscle, Recovery, Endurance, Low Sugar)

---

### 4.2 Predefined Laddoos (Presets)

**Rule:** Every laddoo contains mixed dry fruits/seeds but is dominant in one key ingredient

Required Presets:

1. Mix Dry Fruit Laddoo – Everyday Strength
2. Badam-Dominant Laddoo – Mind & Energy
3. Kaju-Dominant Laddoo – Muscle & Recovery
4. Gond Laddoo – Joint & Warmth
5. Protein-Packed Laddoo – Gym Fuel (with Whey)
6. Omega Recovery Laddoo – Omega-3 focused
7. Endurance Seed Laddoo – High fiber & slow energy
8. Light Energy Makhana Laddoo – Low-fat

Each preset page must show:

* Product images
* Dominant ingredient callout
* Nutrition highlights (protein, calories, key vitamins)
* Fitness benefit tags
* Allergen info

---

### 4.3 Ingredient Transparency

**Goal:** Build trust

Requirements:

* Ingredient cards with:

  * Name
  * 1-line benefit (positive-only)
  * Key vitamins/minerals
  * Fitness tags (muscle, energy, recovery)
* No medical claims
* Simple language

Ingredients to support:

* Nuts & dry fruits
* Seeds
* Bases (besan, aata, goond)
* Fats (ghee, oil)
* Sweeteners (honey, jaggery, sugar, sugar-free)
* Whey protein

---

### 4.4 Build Your Own Laddoo (Configurator)

**Goal:** Fun, intuitive customization

User should be able to:

* Select ingredient quantities (within allowed limits)
* Choose base, fat, and sweetener
* Add whey protein optionally
* Select order quantity (in kg)

UI Requirements:

* Central laddoo visual
* Ingredient sliders or controls
* Live nutrition updates (calories, protein, carbs, fats)
* Live price calculation

System Constraints:

* Enforce min/max limits per ingredient
* Ensure laddoo is physically feasible (binding rules)

---

### 4.5 Nutrition Display

Rules:

* Show nutrition **per 100g** as default
* Optional per-serving view later
* Display:

  * Calories
  * Protein
  * Carbs
  * Fats
* Highlight vitamins/minerals in text (not numeric RDA claims)

Disclaimer required:
"Nutritional values are approximate and for guidance only."

---

### 4.6 Ordering & Pricing

Requirements:

* Quantity selection in kilograms
* Transparent price breakdown:

  * Ingredient cost
  * Preparation fee
  * Packaging
* Lead time messaging (fresh preparation)

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

### 5.1 Design & UX

* Mobile-first
* Highly visual (large images, minimal text)
* Smooth, subtle animations
* No gym clichés or medical visuals

### 5.2 Performance

* Fast page load (<3s on mobile)
* Images optimized for web

### 5.3 Accessibility

* Readable fonts
* Reduced motion option

### 5.4 Deployment & Architecture Constraints (IMPORTANT)

**Hosting**

* Website will be deployed on **GitHub Pages**
* Must be a **fully static site** (HTML + CSS + JavaScript only)
* No backend server, no Node server, no database

**Frontend Requirements**

* Pure JavaScript (Vanilla JS or lightweight framework)
* All state handled client-side
* Nutrition & pricing calculations done in-browser

**Order Collection**

* Orders will be stored via **Google Sheets integration**
* Use one of the following approaches:

  * Google Forms (hidden, auto-filled via JS)
  * Google Apps Script Web App (preferred)

**Security Constraints**

* No sensitive payment data stored
* No API keys exposed directly in frontend JS
* Basic spam protection (honeypot / simple validation)

---

## 6. Compliance & Safety

* FSSAI compliance assumed
* Clear allergen labeling
* No disease cure/prevention claims
* Ingredient-based benefit language only

---

## 7. Out of Scope (For v1)

* Fitness app integrations
* Subscription plans
* Personalized diet recommendations
* Medical nutrition advice

---

## 8. Success Metrics

* Time spent on configurator
* Conversion rate on presets
* Repeat orders
* Saved custom laddoo usage
