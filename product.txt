# Protein & Fitness Laddoo – Concept Artifact

> **Purpose of this document**
> This is a living concept document for a **fitness-focused, customizable laddoo brand**. It captures all raw ingredients and predefined laddoo concepts discussed so far. We will refine this iteratively (copy, nutrition depth, compliance, recipes) before moving to design or implementation.

---

## 1. Brand & Product Vision

**Core Idea**
Traditional, mom-style laddoos reimagined for fitness enthusiasts — with transparent nutrition, protein-forward options, seed-based modern ingredients, and the ability to customize mixes based on goals.

**Positioning**

* Traditional + modern nutrition
* Protein-packed laddoos (plant + whey)
* Clear ingredient-level benefits (positive-only)
* Gym-friendly, clean-label mindset

**Target Audience**

* Gym-goers & fitness enthusiasts
* Health-conscious families
* Corporate gifting (healthy alternatives)
* Athletes looking for convenient calorie/protein snacks

---

## 2. Ingredient Philosophy & Rules

* Only **real, recognizable ingredients**
* Benefits written in **positive, non-medical language** ("supports", "contributes to")
* Nutrition values are **approximate averages**, not medical claims
* Each ingredient tagged by **fitness role** (muscle, energy, recovery, etc.)
* All allergens clearly declared

---

## 4. Predefined Laddoo Concepts (Presets)

> These are **hero products** that users can buy directly or use as starting points for customization.
> **Important rule:** Every laddoo contains a **mix of dry fruits/seeds**, but is **dominant in one key ingredient** that defines its category and benefits.

### 1. Mix Dry Fruit Laddoo – *Everyday Strength*

* Focus: Balanced nutrition from multiple dry fruits
* Key ingredients: Badam, Kaju, Akhrot, Kishmish (balanced proportions)
* Highlights: Protein + healthy fats + natural carbs
* Ideal for: Daily consumption, families, all-age nutrition

### 2. Badam Laddoo – *Mind & Energy*

* Focus: Almond-dominant dry fruit laddoo
* Key ingredients: Badam (dominant) + Kaju, Akhrot, Kishmish (supporting)
* Highlights: Vitamin E, healthy fats, protein
* Ideal for: Mental focus, steady daily energy

### 3. Kaju Laddoo – *Muscle & Recovery*

* Focus: Cashew-dominant nutrition
* Key ingredients: Kaju (dominant) + Badam, Seeds, Besan
* Highlights: Magnesium & iron-rich, calorie-dense
* Ideal for: Muscle recovery, post-workout snacking

### 4. Gond Laddoo – *Joint & Warmth*

* Focus: Traditional strength laddoo
* Key ingredients: Goond (dominant) + Mixed dry fruits
* Highlights: Binding energy, warming nutrition
* Ideal for: Joint comfort, winter nutrition

### 5. Protein-Packed Laddoo – *Gym Fuel*

* Focus: High-protein laddoo
* Key ingredients: Whey (dominant protein) + Seeds + Dry fruits
* Highlights: Protein-rich, BCAA support
* Ideal for: Post-workout recovery, gym-goers

### 6. Omega Recovery Laddoo

* Focus: Healthy fats & omega-3
* Key ingredients: Akhrot, Flax, Chia (dominant fats) + Badam
* Highlights: Omega-3 rich, recovery-friendly
* Ideal for: Active lifestyles, inflammation-conscious diets

### 7. Endurance Seed Laddoo

* Focus: Fiber + slow energy release
* Key ingredients: Chia, Flax, Pumpkin seeds (dominant) + Kishmish
* Highlights: High fiber, sustained energy
* Ideal for: Long workouts, endurance training

### 8. Light Energy Makhana Laddoo

* Focus: Low-fat, clean carbs
* Key ingredients: Makhane (dominant) + Honey + Light dry fruits
* Highlights: Light digestion, quick energy
* Ideal for: Guilt-free snacking

## 5. Nutrition Communication Rules (Draft)

* Show values **per 100 g** as standard
* Optionally show **per laddoo (serving)** later
* Always include disclaimer:
  *"Nutritional values are approximate and based on standard ingredient data."*
* No disease or medical cure claims
