Our Story

From our Punjabi kitchen to your workout bag.

We are a small, family-led collective — the dadis and nanis of Punjabi households — who make laddoos the way we always have: by hand, with patience, and an insistence on spotless kitchens.

Unlike factory-made brands that scale for quantity, we focus on quality, care and honest nutrition. Every batch is a labor of love where tradition meets modern goals: protein-forward options, seed blends for endurance, and natural sweeteners for mindful energy.

“We don’t chase scale. We chase flavour, wellbeing, and the simple joy of seeing somebody smile after one bite.”
